SiS Inflexionpoint (BD) Ltd. (SiSIP) is one of the joint venture initiatives of SiS Inflexionpoint Pte. Ltd, a private equity-structured global supply Chain Company and IP Tech Bangladesh. SISIP is a well-respected company with unique skills, strengths, and leaders that complement each other. SISIP brings in 3 years of experience in Channel Marketing, distribution and investment in technology companies & businesses in Asia. SISIP’s local team brings private equity, more than 2 years of local experience, marketing, sales expertise working capital and financial expertise. A new dimension, a new value offering – Private Equity with distribution and marketing strengths.

Our Mission

Striking just the right balance between where you are now and where you want to be. Between planning and production, between people and technology.
SSIPs’ success lies in honouring the delicate balance between efficient processes and a resourceful team that firmly believes that the road to success is just as important as the destination.
